# LifeVantage Evolve Compensation Plan (2025)

**Category**: Business Opportunity
**Model**: Hybrid Unilevel / Binary (Evolve)

## Core Components

### 1. Customer Sales Profit
- Earn the difference between Wholesale and Retail price on one-time orders.
- Daily pay potential.

### 2. Smart Start Bonus
- Earn up to **40%** on the first order of new Customers and Distributors you enroll.
- paid weekly.
- **Terms** & Qualification: Must maintain minimum Personal Volume (PV).

### 3. Personal Sales Bonus
- Monthly bonus based on your total Customer Sales Volume (CSV).
- **Tier 1**: 500-999 CSV = 5% extra.
- **Tier 2**: 1,000-3,999 CSV = 10% extra.
- **Tier 3**: 4,000+ CSV = 20% extra.

### 4. Sharing Bonus (Mentor Bonus)
- Earn **10%** on the Commissionable Volume (CV) of new Distributors enrolled by your personally enrolled Distributors.
- Helps you earn from helping your team grow.

### 5. Level Commissions (Royalty)
- Earn percentages on the sales volume of your team down multiple levels.
- Depth depends on your Rank (Pro 1 through Executive/Presidential).
- **Dynamic Compression**: Ensures you get paid on volume even if someone above you is inactive.

### 6. Global Bonus Pools
- **Leadership Pool**: Shares of global commissionable volume (4%) split among Executive ranks (Pro 10+).

## Compliance "Safe Harbor" Note
*Earnings are not guaranteed. Success requires hard work, skill, and dedication. The average annual earnings for a typical US Consultant in 2024 was approx $683. Most people join just to buy products at a discount.*
