Bright And Balanced
Get a brilliant solution to brighter-looking skin. TrueScience Perfecting Lotion feels light, fresh, and clean on your skin, but its benefits go deep. This science-backed, ethanol-free formula features Nrf2 technologies to combat oxidative stress, which reduces the visible signs of premature aging. Environmental factors, like the sun and pollution, and your age contribute to skin-cell damage. This can make your skin appear tired, dull, and uneven. Add this simple step to your beauty regimen to improve the appearance of your skin tone, reduce discoloration, and bring back a youthful radiance to your face. More than a toner or serum, Perfecting Lotion elevates your skin care.

Usage
Directions
Smooth an even layer onto clean, dry face and neck, using gentle circular motions. Use AM and PM.
Warnings
For external use only. Keep out of reach of children. Avoid direct contact with eyes. Discontinue use if rash or irritation occurs. If irritation persists, consult your health care practitioner.
