# Reference: Earthwork Productivity Rates

These rates are estimates of how many cubic yards (CY) of material can be moved or processed per hour by various machine types. Use these to calibrate the **Calculator** tab in the Bidding Tool.

---

## 1. Dozers (D6 / D8 Class)
*Primary use: Bulk pushing, grading, and spreading.*

| Soil Type | Pushing Distance < 50ft | Pushing Distance 50-100ft | 100ft+ |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Loose Sand/Dirt** | 250 CY/Hr | 180 CY/Hr | 120 CY/Hr |
| **Compacted Clay** | 180 CY/Hr | 120 CY/Hr | 80 CY/Hr |
| **Caliche/Hardpan** | 100 CY/Hr | 70 CY/Hr | 40 CY/Hr |

---

## 2. Excavators (20-30 Ton Class)
*Primary use: Trenching, loading trucks, and deep excavation.*

| Application | Material | Estimated CY/Hr |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bulk Excavation** | Common Earth | 150 - 200 |
| **Bulk Excavation** | Sticky Clay | 100 - 130 |
| **Trenching (3ft wide)** | Common Earth | 40 - 60 |
| **Loading Dump Trucks** | Any (loose) | 250+ |

---

## 3. Skid Steer / Compact Track Loader (CTL)
*Primary use: Fine grading, tight spaces, and small site prep.*

| Application | Production Rate |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Fine Grading** | 1,500 - 2,500 SqFt / Hr |
| **Backfilling** | 30 - 50 CY / Hr |
| **Spreading Road Base** | 40 - 60 CY / Hr |

---

## 4. Soil "Swell" and "Shrink" Factors
*When you dig dirt up, it expands (Swell). When you compact it, it takes up less space (Shrink).*

| Material | Swell % (Loose) | Shrink % (Compacted) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sand/Gravel** | 10 - 15% | 85 - 95% |
| **Common Earth** | 20 - 25% | 80 - 90% |
| **Clay** | 30 - 35% | 75 - 85% |
| **Rock** | 50 - 60% | 130% (Blasted) |

**Note for Calculator**: If you need 1,000 CY of *compacted* fill, you may need to haul in 1,200 - 1,300 CY of *loose* dirt.

---

## 5. Efficiency Multipliers (The Human Element)
Apply these to the final hour estimate:
*   **Ideal Conditions**: 1.0 (50 min/hr work)
*   **Standard Conditions**: 1.15 (45 min/hr work - "The 45-minute hour")
*   **Poor Conditions**: 1.3 - 1.5 (Rain, mud, tight clearances, overhead obstacles)
