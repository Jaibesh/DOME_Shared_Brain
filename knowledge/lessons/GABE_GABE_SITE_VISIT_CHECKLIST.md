# Checklist: Gabe's Site Visit & Intake

Use this checklist during a site visit to ensure you have all the variables needed for an accurate bid in the Bidding Tool.

---

## 1. Access & Mobilization
- [ ] Can an 18-wheeler with a lowboy trailer get to the site?
- [ ] Are there low hanging power lines or branches?
- [ ] Is there a staging area for equipment and material deliveries?
- [ ] Estimated travel time for mobilization (one way): ________ mins/miles.

## 2. Site Conditions
- [ ] **Soil Identification**: Dig a small hole if possible. (Sand / Clay / Rock / Caliche?)
- [ ] **Vegetation**: How many acres need clearing? Any trees larger than 12" diameter?
- [ ] **Water/Drainage**: Where does water naturally sit? Will we need to install culverts or swales?
- [ ] **Utilities**: Search for gas markers, electric boxes, or private septic/well heads. (Remind client of 811).

## 3. Project Dimensions & Scope
- [ ] Measure total project area (SqFt or Acres).
- [ ] Estimate average depth of material to be moved/added.
- [ ] **Material Source**: Can we "cut and fill" (move dirt from one part of the site to another) or do we need to haul in/out?
- [ ] **Finish Requirements**: Does the pad need structural compaction testing? Does the site require seed/straw after grading?

## 4. Photos to Take
- [ ] Wide shots of the entire site from 4 corners.
- [ ] Close up of the soil.
- [ ] Entry/Exit points for equipment.
- [ ] Any existing structures or hazards.

## 5. Client Expectations
- [ ] Desired start date: ________________
- [ ] Desired completion date: ________________
- [ ] Budget range discussed? (Y/N) ________
- [ ] Is an engineer or permit required before we start?

---

**Pro Tip**: Fill out the **Project Intake** tab in the Google Sheet *immediately* after the site visit while the details are fresh.
