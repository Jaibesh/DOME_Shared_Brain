Frequently Asked Questions
Does TrueScience Liquid Collagen work?
Yes. Cell studies have shown Liquid Collagen activates multiple key genes involved in increasing collagen and supporting skin health. Additionally, in an 8-week clinical study, participants saw decreased crow’s feet depth, reduced appearance of pores, improved skin moisture, brightness, and smoothness. Results may vary because skin turnover takes at least 28 days–even longer as we age.*,¶¶
What does TrueScience Liquid Collagen do?
Liquid Collagen does more than replenish collagen through supplementation. It’s scientifically shown to activate your body to make 5 types of its own collagen, maintain existing collagen by slowing down collagen-degrading enzymes, and increase collagen density by 100%. Benefits extend to skin, hair, nails, joints, and connective tissue.*,◊◊,##
Why liquid collagen instead of a powder?
Liquid is easily digestible and absorbable. The most important factor isn’t the form–it’s the type of collagen. Hydrolyzed collagen peptides are already broken down into smaller chains, making them easier for your body to absorb and use. TrueScience® Liquid Collagen delivers these hydrolyzed peptides in a convenient, great-tasting drink. Many people also prefer liquid because it’s pre-mixed, portable, and enjoyable, which makes daily consistency easier.
What makes Liquid Collagen unique?
The unique blend of marine collagen paired with red quinoa and antioxidant-rich botanicals works in 3 ways to activate, replenish, and maintain collagen. Unlike most supplements that only provide 1 or 2 types of collagen, Liquid Collagen includes peptides from 10 collagen types. It’s also clinically shown to activate your body to make 5 different collagen types.*, ##
Do I really need to take collagen?
Your collagen production begins to decline after age 25. You need a broad range of the right collagen types to ensure your skin and connective tissues for your bones and joints get what they need to look and feel healthy.*
