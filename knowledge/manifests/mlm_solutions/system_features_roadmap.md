# System Features Roadmap: "The Digital Upline" 2.0

> [!NOTE]
> Based on research into 2025 trends (Fraser Brookes, Eric Worre), the ultimate MLM system combines **High-Tech Automation** with **High-Touch Community**.

## 1. The Core Philosophy
**"Simplicity Scales, Complexity Fails."**
Our goal is not to give them *more* tools, but to give them a *single* workflow that works on a phone.

## 3. "Must-Have" Features for 2025 (Research Backed)

### A. The "Gamified" Onboarding (Skool Model)
*   **The Problem:** New distributors feel lost.
*   **The Feature:** A "Level 1" unlocked course. They only unlock "Level 2" (Advanced Tactics) after they prove they made their first 3 posts.
*   **Tech:** Skool is the leader here. It replaces the "boring LMS" with a game.

### B. "One-Tap" Duplication (Systeme Model)
*   **The Problem:** "I can't build a website."
*   **The Feature:** A "Share Logic" link. The leader builds the funnel -> clicks Share -> The new distributor clicks -> It is instantly managing their leads.
*   **Validation:** Confirmed as a top feature for 2025 MLM software.

### C. The "AI Content Assistant" (Fraser Brookes Strategy)
*   **The Problem:** "I don't know what to say."
*   **The Feature:** A weekly "Drop" of 5 pre-written scripts + 5 "B-Roll" video clips.
*   **The automation:** The leader uploads raw video -> AI chops it -> Team gets the clips.

### D. Compliance "Guard Rails"
*   **The Problem:** Getting sued.
*   **The Feature:** All shared assets are pre-vetted.
*   **Implementation:** A "Safe Vault" (like our `scripts_vault.md`) where distributors are told: *"Only post what is in here."*

## 4. The Implementation Phases

### Phase 1: Validated MVP (Weeks 1-4)
*   **Platform:** Systeme.io (Free).
*   **Asset:** The "Biohacking Funnel" (Completed).
*   **Training:** A simple PDF or unlisted YouTube playlist.
*   **Goal:** Get 10 teammates to launch the funnel.

### Phase 2: Community Migration (Month 2)
*   **Platform:** Move training to **Skool**.
*   **Feature:** Launch "The 30-Day Biohack Challenge" (Leaderboard for most leads generated).
*   **Goal:** Create culture and retention.

### Phase 3: The "Super App" (Month 6+)
*   **Platform:** White-label GoHighLevel app.
*   **Feature:** A custom mobile app where they can text leads, see stats, and watch training all in one place.
*   **Goal:** Enterprise scale.
