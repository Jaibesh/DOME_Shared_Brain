# Content Machine Workflow: The "One-to-Many" Engine

> [!NOTE]
> This workflow turns **1 hour** of work into **1 month** of content. The goal is consistency without burnout.

## 1. The Strategy: "Reverse Pyramid" 
We start with one heavy lift (Pillar Content) and slice it down using AI.

**Theme**: "The Science of Anti-Aging" (Example)

---

## 2. Pillar Content Creation (1x / Week)
**Format**: Long-form Video (YouTube / Zoom Recording) - 10-20 Minutes.
**Action**: Record yourself explaining a concept from your specific LifeVantage research (e.g., "How Nrf2 works", "Why Collagen fails", "Interview with a Upline Leader").
**Tool**: OBS, Zoom, or simply your Phone.

---

## 3. The "Chop Shop" Workflow (Automated)

### Step 1: Transcribe & Blog (5 Mins)
-   **Input**: Upload video file to **Descript** or **Otter.ai**.
-   **Action**: Generate Transcript.
-   **AI Prompt (ChatGPT/Claude)**: "Take this transcript and write a 1,000-word SEO-optimized blog post titled [Topic]. Use H2 headers."
-   **Output**: 1 Blog Post / LinkedIn Article.

### Step 2: Vertical Video Clips (10 Mins)
-   **Input**: Upload original video link to **OpusClip** (or similar AI clipper).
-   **Action**: "Auto-Detect Highlights".
-   **Output**: 5-10 Viral Shorts (vertical format with burned-in captions).
-   **Distribution**: TikTok, IG Reels, YouTube Shorts.

### Step 3: Social Text Posts (5 Mins)
-   **Input**: The Blog Post from Step 1.
-   **AI Prompt**: "Extract 5 contrarian or punchy quotes from this text. Format them for Twitter/X threads and LinkedIn text-only posts."
-   **Output**: 10 Text posts (Quotes, Threads, Stories).

### Step 4: Visual Carousels (10 Mins)
-   **Input**: The "Key Points" from the text.
-   **Tool**: **Canva** (Bulk Create Feature).
-   **Action**: Create a "Carousel" template. Paste the 5 key points.
-   **Output**: 1 Instagram Carousel (PDF for LinkedIn).

---

## 4. The Distribution Schedule (Weekly Rhythm)

| Day | Platform A (IG/TikTok) | Platform B (YouTube) | Platform C (Email/List) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mon** | Short #1 (The Hook) | - | Email: "New Video/Post" |
| **Tue** | Carousel (Education) | - | - |
| **Wed** | Short #2 (Objection) | Pillar Video Upload | - |
| **Thu** | Quote/Static Image | - | Email: Value/Story |
| **Fri** | Short #3 (Lifestyle) | - | - |
| **Sat** | Reel (Trending Audio) | - | - |
| **Sun** | Personal/Family Story | - | Weekly Round-up |

**Total Output**: 3 Shorts, 1 Video, 1 Blog, 2 Emails, 3 Static Posts.
**Total Work Time**: ~2 Hours (1hr Record + 1hr AI Processing).
