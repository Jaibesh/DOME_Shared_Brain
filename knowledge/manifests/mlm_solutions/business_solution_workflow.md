# LifeVantage Business Solution Workflow

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This workflow is designed to optimize the "Evolve Compensation Plan" (2024-2025) by leveraging modern automation and strategic "Agentic" workflows. Success requires consistent execution of the Daily Method of Operation (DMO).

## 1. Executive Summary
**Objective:** Create a scalable, high-efficiency business model for LifeVantage that maximizes the "Share, Build, Lead" pillars of the Evolve plan.
**Core Strategy:** Move away from manual "warm market" harassment and towards **Attraction Marketing** and **Automated Systems**.

---

## 2. Phase 1: Foundation & Compliance (Days 1-7)
*Focus: "Share" Pillar - Establishing the baseline for commissions.*

### 2.1 Strategic Setup
- **Enrollment**: Secure the "Elite Pack" (if budget permits) for maximum product on hand or "Start Kit".
- **Subscription Lock**: Set up a monthly subscription for **150 SV** (Sales Volume) to guarantee "Active" status. 
    - *Recommendation*: Personal use of Protandim Nrf2 + TrueScience Liquid Collagen to meet the ~40 PV personal requirement and have samples.
- **Link Configuration**: Configure the personalized LifeVantage referral site.

### 2.2 Product Knowledge "Deep Dive"
- Master the science of **Nutrigenomics** and Nrf2 activation.
- **Key Selling Points**:
    - "Flip the switch" on aging (don't just supplement).
    - Peer-reviewed studies (PubMed references).
    - Protandim reduces oxidative stress by 40% in 30 days.

## 3. Phase 2: The "Smart" Lead Generation Engine
*Focus: "Build" Pillar - Automating the influx of prospects.*

Instead of chasing friends/family, we build a **Biohacking Funnel**.

### 3.1 Target Audience Avatars
1.  **The Optimizer**: Interested in longevity, biohacking, and high performance.
2.  **The Side-Hustler**: Looking for a second income stream (low barrier to entry).
3.  **The Skincare Enthusiast**: Interested in science-backed beauty (TrueScience).

### 3.2 Automated Content Workflow
**Goal**: Drive traffic to your referral link/landing page.
- **Platforms**: TikTok/IG Reels (Short form), YouTube (Educational).
- **Content Pillars**:
    - *Education*: "What is Oxidative Stress?" "Why Antioxidants aren't enough."
    - *Lifestyle*: Showing the "Freedom" aspect of the business.
    - *Testimonial*: Sharing personal or team results (Compliance compliant).

### 3.3 The Funnel Structure
1.  **Traffic Source** (Social Media/Ads) ->
2.  **Lead Magnet** (Free PDF: "Top 5 Biohacks for 2025" or "Anti-Aging Checklist") ->
3.  **Opt-in Page** (Capture Email/Phone) ->
4.  **Bridge Page** (Video introducing YOU and the LifeVantage opportunity) ->
5.  **Call to Action** (Link to LifeVantage Shop or "Book a Strategy Call").

### 4.2 The "Sharing Bonus" Strategy

## 5. Daily Method of Operation (DMO) Checklist

### Morning (30 Mins)
- [ ] **Personal Development**: Read/Listen to MLM mindset or nutrigenomics research.
- [ ] **Health Check**: Take products (Be a product of the product).
- [ ] **Post**: Upload 1 piece of content to Social Media (recorded previously).

### Mid-Day (15 Mins - "Pockets of Time")
- [ ] **Engage**: Reply to comments on posts.
- [ ] **Outreach**: Send 3-5 specific DMs to people who engaged with your content (NOT cold DMs).
    - *Script*: "Hey [Name], saw you liked the post about oxidative stress. Are you currently using any Nrf2 activators?"

### Evening (45 Mins)
- [ ] **Follow-up**: Check CRM/Lead list. Follow up with anyone in the funnel.
- [ ] **Team Connection**: 15-min check-in with key downline leaders.
- [ ] **Creation**: Batched content creation for the next day.
