# Evolve Compensation Plan - Research Summary

## Plan Overview
**Launch**: March 2023, Full effect May 2024
**Structure**: Share → Build & Grow → Lead

---

## Consultant Ranks

| Rank | Requirements | Key Benefits |
|------|--------------|--------------|
| Consultant | Enrollment | Base commission access |
| Senior Consultant 1 (SC1) | Sales + team metrics | Doubled Sharing Bonus eligible |
| EC1-EC4 | Progressive team building | Leadership bonuses, perks |
| Presidential | Top rank | Maximum compensation, travel |

---

## Bonus Structure

### Customer Sales Bonuses

| Tier | CSV Required | Bonus % |
|------|--------------|---------|
| Onyx | 500-999 SV | 5% |
| Emerald | 1,000-1,999 SV | 10% |
| Sapphire | 2,000-3,999 SV | 15% |
| Diamond | 4,000+ SV | 20% |

### Key Bonuses

1. **Sharing Bonus**: 10% commission on new enrollees' purchases
2. **Doubled Sharing Bonus**: 20% if SC1 achieved in first 4 months
3. **Customer Sales Profit**: Difference between customer/consultant pricing (daily)
4. **Rank Advancement Bonus**: Fast Track Period achievement rewards

---

## Fast Track Period Goals
- Enrollment month + 3 calendar months
- Enroll someone within 30 days
- First customer sale within 7 days
- Setup 150 SV+ subscription

---

## Evolve Perks (2025)

### Rank Maintenance Gifts
- **EC1-EC4**: Maintain rank 4/12 months = Meta Quest VR Headset (2025)
- **Presidential**: Maintain 8/12 months = Premium rewards

### My LifeVenture (EC2+)
- Personalized video feature
- Choice: $30,000 toward Jeep Wrangler OR $25,000 cash
- Requires: EC2 rank + maintain 6 consecutive months within 18 months

---

## Activity Requirements

| Requirement | Amount |
|-------------|--------|
| Personal Volume (PV) | ~40 PV minimum |
| Active Status | 150 SV monthly subscription |
| Recommended Products | Protandim Nrf2 + TrueScience Collagen |

---

## Income Pathways

1. **Direct Sales Only**: Focus on customer acquisition, CSV bonuses
2. **Team Building**: Sharing bonuses + rank advancement
3. **Hybrid**: Balance both for maximum earnings

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The plan is designed to reward NEW consultants quickly via Fast Track bonuses.
> Subscriptions (Rewards Circle) are critical for customer/team retention.
