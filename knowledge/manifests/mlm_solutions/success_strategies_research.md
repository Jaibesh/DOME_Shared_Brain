## Digital Marketing Strategies

### Content That Works
1. **Educational**: "What is Oxidative Stress?" "Why Antioxidants Fail"
2. **Lifestyle**: Freedom, flexibility, wellness journey
3. **Testimonial**: Personal/team results (compliance-safe)
4. **Viral Hooks**: Short-form video with visual demos

### Platform Strategy

| Platform | Content Type | Best Practices |
|----------|--------------|----------------|
| TikTok | 7-15s hooks | Trending audio, visual demos (apple oxidation) |
| IG Reels | Educational shorts | Carousels, before/after |
| YouTube | Long-form education | Pillar content, SEO |

## Daily Method of Operation (DMO)

### Morning (30 min)
- [ ] Personal development (MLM mindset, nutrigenomics)
- [ ] Take products (be a product of the product)
- [ ] Post 1 piece of pre-batched content

### Mid-Day (15 min)
- [ ] Reply to comments
- [ ] Send 3-5 DMs to engaged prospects (NOT cold)
- [ ] Script: "Hey [Name], saw you liked the post about X. Are you currently using...?"

### Evening (45 min)
- [ ] Follow up with CRM leads
- [ ] 15-min team check-in
- [ ] Batch content for tomorrow
